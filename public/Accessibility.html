Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

AutomateHub Studio is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We strive to continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

  • We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.
  • We regularly review our website to identify and fix accessibility issues.
  • We provide alternative text for images and use semantic HTML for better screen reader support.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@automatehubstudio.com

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvements and welcome your feedback to help us achieve our goal of an inclusive digital experience for all users.
